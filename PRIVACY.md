# Aperio Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 3, 2026*

Aperio ("the app") is a Bible reading and study app. We built it to respect your privacy by design.

## What the app collects

**Nothing, by default.** You can read scripture, study commentary, search, highlight, bookmark, and keep a prayer journal without creating an account. All of that data is stored only on your device. The app's scripture, commentary, and lexicon content is bundled inside the app, so reading requires no internet connection and sends no data anywhere.

## If you choose to sign in (optional)

If you sign in with Apple or Google, we store:

- Your account identifier and email address (as provided by Apple or Google)
- Your bookmarks, highlights, and prayer journal entries, so they can be backed up and restored across your devices

This data is stored with our backend provider, Supabase, and is protected so that only your account can read or write your own data. We do not sell, share, or use this data for advertising or analytics. There are no ads and no trackers in Aperio.

## Deleting your data

In the app, go to Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all synced data from our servers. Data saved on your device remains yours and can be removed by deleting the app.

## Children

Aperio does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app can be used entirely without an account.
